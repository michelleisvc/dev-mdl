Signal in the Noise: Early Thoughts on AI Agents

Date: December 01, 2025

Spent the morning synthesizing how agent-native workflows reshape expectations around trust and autonomy. Noting early patterns that feel similar to the shift from on-prem to cloud: once people feel the convenience, the baseline expectations change permanently.

Patterns from Founder Conversations

Date: November 30, 2025

Had a few calls with technical founders building infra for multimodal models. The biggest consistent pain point: identity and permissioning for agents operating across tools. Everyone feels the gap, no one believes the existing stack solves it.

Notes on Market Momentum

Date: November 29, 2025

Rough draft of a framework to track demand signals across B2B use cases. Early adopters are clustering in finance, e-commerce ops, and customer support—segments where speed-to-action matters more than perfect accuracy.

Reflections on Trust & Safety Themes

Date: November 28, 2025

Realized many historical trust frameworks assume static systems and human gatekeepers. Agents break both assumptions. Thinking through how those mental models need to evolve if autonomy becomes the default.
